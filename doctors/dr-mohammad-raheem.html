Clinical Biography

Dr. Raheem Mohammad, BDS, MDS

Consultant Endodontist & Restorative Dentist

Dr. Raheem Mohammad is a highly skilled dental specialist with expertise in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics. He completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Government Dental College, Vijayawada and earned his Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics from Vishnu Dental College.

He is a proud member of the Indian Association of Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics and is committed to delivering evidence-based, patient-centered dental care.

His areas of expertise include advanced root canal treatments, retreatment of failed root canals, management of dental pain and infections, esthetic restorations, and preservation of natural teeth.

At Apex Dental Hospital, Dr. Raheem focuses on providing precise diagnosis, painless treatment, and personalized care using modern dental techniques and strict sterilization protocols.

His goal is to help every patient achieve optimal oral health while ensuring a comfortable and positive treatment experience.

“Dedicated to preserving natural teeth and creating healthy, confident smiles.”
